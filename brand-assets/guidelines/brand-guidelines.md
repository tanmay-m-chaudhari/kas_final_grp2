# ShopPilot Brand Guidelines

## Brand Overview

ShopPilot is a modern e-commerce platform focused on simplicity, speed, and trust.

## Logo Usage

- Minimum size: 32px height
- Clear space: equal to the height of the "S" letterform on all sides
- Do not stretch, rotate, or recolour the logo without approval

## Color Palette

| Name       | Hex       | Usage                  |
|------------|-----------|------------------------|
| Ocean Blue | #1A6CF4   | Primary CTA, links     |
| Slate Dark | #1E293B   | Headings, body text    |
| Cloud Gray | #F1F5F9   | Backgrounds, cards     |
| Coral      | #F43F5E   | Error states, badges   |
| Mint       | #10B981   | Success, confirmation  |

## Typography

- **Headings**: Inter, weight 700
- **Body**: Inter, weight 400
- **Monospace**: JetBrains Mono (code snippets, SKUs)

## Voice & Tone

- Clear and direct — no jargon
- Friendly but professional
- Always customer-first language
